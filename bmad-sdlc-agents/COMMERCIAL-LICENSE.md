## Who needs a commercial license?

You need a commercial license if **any** of the following are true:

1. You are a for-profit company, or you are using BMAD on behalf of one.
2. You are deploying BMAD inside a commercial product, internal tool, or service that generates revenue (directly or indirectly).
3. You are an independent contractor or consultancy using BMAD to deliver paid work for a client.
4. You are bundling, redistributing, or rebranding BMAD in any commercial software, SaaS, or managed service.
5. You are training employees, contractors, or partners on BMAD as part of a paid engagement.

You do **not** need a commercial license if you are:

- An individual using BMAD on personal projects, hobby work, study, or open-source contributions with no commercial intent.
- A non-profit, charity, school, university, or government body using BMAD for its mission.
- A researcher publishing under standard academic terms.
- An employee evaluating BMAD on a personal machine before any organizational adoption (evaluation period: up to 30 days).
